📦 Get cookies manually (no browser needed)

  1. Open linkedin.com/feed in any browser (your laptop is fine).
  2. Log in normally with email + password + 2FA.
  3. Press F12 → open Application tab → Cookies → click https://www.linkedin.com.
  4. Find the li_at row → double-click its value → Ctrl+C.
  5. Paste into "Option B" above → click "Save & Test".

✅ No browser automation needed, no Playwright required, works from any country.
